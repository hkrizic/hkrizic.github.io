Open a math or physics PDF to begin.

Hover over equation references like (3.1) or theorem references like Theorem 5.1 to peek at the source — pulled straight from the page so the math typography is preserved exactly. Click a reference to jump to it.

Once a PDF is open, the toolbar above gives you zoom, page navigation, and a Note tool: click + Note then click anywhere on a page to drop a sticky note. Notes accept inline math with $x^2$ and display math with $$\int_0^1 x\,dx$$ — rendered live by KaTeX. Notes are saved locally per file.

Works best on hyperref'd LaTeX PDFs. Scanned PDFs have no text/links to detect.
