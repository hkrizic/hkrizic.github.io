In the heart of Dubrovnik — the Pearl of the Adriatic

Address

Od Srđa 22, Dubrovnik

Villa Antipodes sits on the hillside above Dubrovnik's famed Old Town, on the quiet residential street of Od Srđa. From here, you are just a short walk downhill to the ancient city walls, Stradun promenade, and the sparkling Adriatic Sea.

The villa's elevated position rewards guests with sweeping views over the terracotta rooftops of the Old Town, across to the green island of Lokrum, and up to Mount Srd. The cable car station is within walking distance, making it easy to take in sunset panoramas from the summit.

A Note About the Walk

Villa Antipodes is reached via a charming stone pathway typical of Dubrovnik's hillside neighborhoods. While the walk is uphill and can be steep in places, guests consistently say the stunning views and perfect location make it absolutely worthwhile.
