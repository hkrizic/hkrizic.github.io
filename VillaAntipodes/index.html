Welcome

Your Home in the Pearl of the Adriatic

Villa Antipodes is a beautifully renovated Mediterranean villa nestled in the hillside above Dubrovnik's iconic Old Town. With its white stone walls, sun-drenched terraces, and panoramic sea views, it offers the perfect retreat for travelers seeking authentic Croatian charm with modern luxury.

Just a five-minute walk from the ancient city walls, the villa blends effortless elegance with an unbeatable location — your gateway to the wonders of Dubrovnik.
