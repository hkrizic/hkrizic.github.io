Elegance Meets Comfort

Villa Antipodes accommodates up to 6 guests across 3 beautifully appointed bedrooms, with spacious living areas, a designer kitchen, and a sun-drenched terrace overlooking Dubrovnik.

The open-plan living area is flooded with natural light and furnished with a mix of contemporary and classic Mediterranean pieces. Relax on the comfortable sofa, enjoy the flat-screen TV, or gather at the dining table for a meal together.
